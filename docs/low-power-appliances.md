# Low Power Appliance Policy

This project enforces a fixed per-outlet cap of 500W at 230V (PH mains).
Combined load should stay under 1000W when both outlets are active.

## Allowed (low power, under 500W)

Personal devices
- Phone chargers
- Tablet chargers
- Laptop chargers
- Smartwatch chargers
- Wireless earbuds charging case

Entertainment
- LED TVs
- Gaming consoles (PS4, PS5, Xbox, Nintendo Switch dock)
- Streaming devices (Chromecast, Fire Stick)
- Soundbars
- Small Bluetooth speakers

Lighting
- LED lamps
- LED desk lamps
- LED strip lights

Cooling
- Electric fans (desk, stand, wall)
- Small USB-powered fans

Office or study
- Desktop monitors
- Routers or modems
- Raspberry Pi or small computers
- Small CCTV cameras

Other small appliances
- Electric clocks
- Small radios
- Night lights

## Caution (verify actual wattage)
- Small printers (laser printers can exceed 500W)
- Small UPS units (charging and inrush can exceed 500W)

## Not supported (high power or high inrush)
- Air conditioners
- Refrigerators
- Washing machines
- Electric kettles
- Microwave ovens
- Electric irons
- Hair dryers
